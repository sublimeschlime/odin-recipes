Dakdoritang

Description

A Spicy Korean Chicken Stew Dish. Intense flavors from gochujang and gochugaru.

Ingredients

For the Rice

  • Rice

For the Stew

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Thigh
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • Russet Potato
  • Minced Garlic
  • Green Onion
  • Chicken Stock
  • Oil

For the Sauce

  • Gochujang
  • Gochugaru
  • Honey
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cornstarch Slurry

Steps

  1. Cook Rice
  2. Wash and cut all vegetables
  3. Cut carrots, celery, and potatoes into a large dice
  4. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  5. In a large stock pot over medium high heat add oil and veggies
  6. Add chicken
