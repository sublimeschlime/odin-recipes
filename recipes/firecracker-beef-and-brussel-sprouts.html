Firecracker Beef and Brussel Sprouts

Description

Recipe from the meal prep manual of a spicy and sweet ground beef with brussel sprouts

Ingredients

For the Rice

  • Rice

For the Beef and Brussels

  • Ground Beef
  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Onion
  • Poblano Peppers
  • Minced Garlic
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Green Onions (optional)
  • Lime (optional)
  • Oil

For the Sauce

  • Hot Sauce
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Honey
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Wash and cut vegetables
  3. Cut stems off brussel sprouts and remove damaged outer layers
  4. Cut onions, green onions, and peppers into a small dice
  5. Place brussel sprouts into a large bowl and drizzle oil
  6. Preheat airfryer to 400 degrees
  7. Place brussels into airfryer for 5-8 minutes
  8. In a large skillet over medium high heat add beef
  9. After half way cooked add vegetables
  10. Prepare sauce by mixing all ingredients
  11. When beef is 90% of the way done add sauce
  12. Serve
